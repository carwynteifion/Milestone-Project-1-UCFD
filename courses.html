Our Courses

Beginner Course

Totally new to Welsh? Or wanting to brush up on the basics? This course is the one for you! Learn to introduce yourself, learn colours, count to 20 and say short, simple sentences. We even have quizzes for you to test your knowledge!

Intermediate Course

This course is perfect if you have completed the Beginner course and want to continue your learning journey, or even if you already understand basic Welsh and want to jump right in. Here you'll learn conversational Welsh, different dialects, and become confident in day-to-day conversation. Try the quizzes to see how much you have learned!

Advanced Course

The big leagues! Choose this course if you have a good understanding of Welsh and want to be a Welsh-speaking champion! Learn all about mutations, become confident in writing in Welsh, and take our test to become certified. Perfect if you are considering a career involving the Welsh language.

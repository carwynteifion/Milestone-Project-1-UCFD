Why Learn Welsh?

It's the oldest language in Europe

Welsh has been spoken in Britain since at least 600BC. Alongside Cornish and Breton, it's one of the Brythonic languages, and is older than the Parthenon, Socrates and the Roman Empire!

Start a new career

With a higher demand than ever for Welsh language skills in the workplace, in schools and in government offices, there's no better time to learn.

It's spoken by 500,000 people globally

Welsh isn't just spoken in Wales. Join a global community of Welsh speakers, from Pennsylvania to Patagonia!

Impress your friends

Be the life of the party as the person who knows the Welsh word for microwave, or the one who can pronounce that really long place name!
